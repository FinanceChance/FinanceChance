Data Removal

We take your data privacy seriously. If you wish to remove your personal information from our system, please follow the steps below:

  1. Enter your email address in the field below.
  2. Click the "Remove Data" button to initiate the data removal process.
  3. You will receive a confirmation email with further instructions.


Note: By requesting data removal, you understand that certain types of non-identifiable data may still be retained for statistical purposes in an aggregated format.
